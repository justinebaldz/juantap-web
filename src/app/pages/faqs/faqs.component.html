how_to_reg How do I register my Juantap card?

Step 1: You need to purchase the Juantap Card.

Step 2: Go visit our website, Juantap then click the 'Register' button.

Step 3: You can now enter your personal and account information. To validate your email, double-check that your email address is spelled correctly or that it exist. Then, to submit your information, click 'Create.'
On the front of your purchased card, you'll see your Card Account Number.

Step 4: It will then ask for your verification code, that will be sent to the email address you used to register. Every minute, you can resend an OTP.

Step 5: After registering your Juantap card, you may now log in to check your account, monitor your card balance, and transactions, and also login to our mobile app to unlock additional features.


You haven't registered yet? and wanted to see how much amount was left on your card?

To view your card balance, simply click the 'Quick View' button in the Login Panel and input your Card Account Number.

 pin How to Log in?

Make sure that your Juantap card is already registered.

Step 1: Enter your email address or phone number, as well as your password, and then click the 'Login' button.

Step 2: You can now see your card balance, account information, and track your transaction after logging in. You can also login in our mobile app.

 account_circle Why should you register your Juantap card?

The advantages of registering your Juantap card include the ability to check and monitor your balance and transactions, as well as unlocking additional features in our mobile app.

 payments How to cash-in?

An easy step to cash in:

Step 1: You must buy a Juantap card at the nearest jeepney terminal that uses our system.

Step 2: Request cash-in by presenting your Juantap card to the cashier.

The smallest amount of cash you can load to your Juantap card is 10 pesos.

Step 3: After that, you can make a secure transaction with your Juantap card.
